On-Page SEO: Internal Optimization

On-page SEO refers to the practice of optimizing individual web pages to rank higher and earn more relevant traffic in search engines[cite: 15]. It involves both the content of a page and the HTML source code[cite: 15].

Key Elements:

  • Title Tags: Every page should have a unique title tag that includes the main keyword[cite: 15].
  • Meta Descriptions: A brief summary of the page that appears in search results to improve click-through rates[cite: 15].
  • Heading Tags: Using H1 tags for titles and H2/H3 for subheadings helps search engines understand content hierarchy[cite: 15].
  • Internal Linking: Linking to other pages on your own site helps search engines discover new content[cite: 15].

Quick Tip

Always ensure your images have "Alt Text" for better accessibility and SEO[cite: 15].
