On-Page SEO: Internal Optimization

Optimizing the elements you control directly on your site.

The Art of On-Page Optimization

On-page SEO involves ensuring that both the content and technical elements of your page are optimized for search engines and users. It involves both the content of a page and the HTML source code.

Key Elements:

  • Title Tags: Every page should have a unique title tag that includes the main keyword.
  • Meta Descriptions: A brief summary of the page that appears in search results to improve click-through rates.
  • Heading Tags: Using H1 tags for titles and H2/H3 for subheadings helps search engines understand content hierarchy.
  • Internal Linking: Linking to other pages on your own site helps search engines discover new content.

Quick Tip

Always ensure your images have "Alt Text" for better accessibility and SEO image search ranking.
