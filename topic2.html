Off-Page SEO: Building Reputation

Off-page SEO includes activities performed outside of a website to improve its ranking within search engine results pages (SERPs)[cite: 16]. It is primarily about building the site's authority and trustworthiness in the eyes of others[cite: 16].

The Power of Backlinks

Backlinks are the most critical component of off-page SEO. When a high-authority website links to yours, it acts as a "vote of confidence," signaling to search engines that your content is valuable[cite: 16].

  • Natural Links: Links given by other site owners who find your content helpful[cite: 16].
  • Outreach: Contacting influencers or bloggers to feature your content[cite: 16].
