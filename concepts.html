The SEO Glossary

Building a technical foundation for search engine success.

Essential Vocabulary

SERP (Search Engine Results Page)
The page displayed by a search engine in response to a query by a user.
Crawling
The discovery process in which search engines send out a team of robots (known as crawlers or spiders) to find new and updated content.
Indexing
The process where search engines store and organize the information they find during crawling.
Keywords
The specific words and phrases that users enter into search engines to find information.
Organic Results
Non-paid search results determined purely by the search engine's algorithm.
